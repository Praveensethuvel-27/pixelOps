Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: December 27, 2025

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Communicate with us via email or chat

This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and project details.

2. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to:

Your information is used solely to:

4. Data Security

We take security seriously:

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Contact us at info@pixelops.com for any requests.

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics, email delivery) that have their own privacy policies. We only share necessary information and ensure they maintain high privacy standards.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.
